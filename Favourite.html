Favourite Writer

Ruskin Bond

Ruskin Bond, an eminent Indian author, was born on May 19, 1934, in Kasauli, Himachal Pradesh. His enchanting stories, set against the backdrop of the picturesque Himalayas, have captivated readers worldwide. With a literary career spanning several decades, Bond is celebrated for his ability to weave magic into everyday tales.

Explore the world of Ruskin Bond and delve into his rich literary legacy that transports readers to the tranquil landscapes of his narratives.
